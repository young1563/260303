Key Takeaways for Developers

  • Start with Accessibility: Maintain the hyper-casual 'instant fun' factor in the first 30 seconds.
  • Layer Complexity Gradually: Introduce meta-systems (gear, cards, social) only after the core loop is mastered.
  • Diversify Revenue: Don't rely solely on ads. Design systems that make players *want* to purchase, even if it's small amounts (micro-transactions).
  • Data-Driven Iteration: Use D30 and IAP conversion as your North Star metrics, not just D1 installs.
